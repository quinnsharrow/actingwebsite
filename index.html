Biography

Quinn Sharrow is a Los Angeles based actor, military veteran, college graduate, hard worker, and fierce friend. He grew up in West Virginia and later joined the U.S. Army. After denying that inner voice for so long, he finally chose to follow his heart and dedicate his life to an acting career in 2013. He sought out an agent. He sought out training. He sought out SAG elibigility. He got them. Nothing can stop him once a goal is set.
